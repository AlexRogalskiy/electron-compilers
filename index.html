electron-compilers

electron-compilers are the actual implementations of classes that power electron-compile

For JavaScript:

  • JavaScript ES6/ES7 (via Babel)
  • TypeScript
  • CoffeeScript

For CSS:

  • LESS

Why is this a separate repo?!

Shipping compilers for all of these languages will add a ton of weight to your download size. Making this a separate top-level module makes it super easy to delete it all in one go from the final production result.
